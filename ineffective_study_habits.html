INEFFECTIVE STUDY HABITS AND SOLUTIONS

Ineffective study habits is a barrier for many students and adults, it can be whether they have distractions, procrastinations, cramming, or the lack of amount of sleep they get etc. This website will allow studengts and adults to figure out what ineffective study habits they have and what solutions will allow them to fix their ineffective habits and turn them into effective study habits.

LIST OF INEFFECTIVE STUDY HABITS:

- Studying without a plan: When studying without a plan there will be a lack of direction which will cause students and adults to find it difficult to cover all their subjects and work within the limited amount of time they have.

- Cramming: Many people decide to leave their work til last minute, which can affect them badly, causing them to study everything before the due date.

- Distractions: It is very common for people to get distracted whenever it comes to studying due to social media, family, and friends, this habit can affect people to delay their studies and become behind in classes.
