MOTIVATIONAL STUDY QUOTES

"The more that you read, the more things you will know, the more that you learn, the more places you'll go." - Dr. Seuss.

"Do not let what you cannot do interfere with what you can do." - John Wooden.

"If you really want to do something, you'll find a way. If you don't, you'll find an excuse." - Jim Rohn.

"Everybody is a genius. But if you judge a fish by its ability to climb a tree, it will spend its whole life believing that it is stupid." - Albert Einstein.

"There are no secrets to success. It is the result of preparation, hard work, and learning from failure." - General Colin Powell.

"Success consists of going from failure to failure without loss of enthusiasm." - Winston Churchill.

"The best way to predict your future is to create it." - Abraham Lincoln.

"You may encounter many defeats but you must not be defeated. In fact, it may be necessary to encounter the defeats, so you can know who you are, what you can rise from, how you can still come out of it." - Maya Angelou.

"Nobody can go back and start a new beginning, but anyone can start today and make a new ending." - Maria Robinson.

"Believe in yurself, take on your challenges, dig deep within yourself to conquer fears. Never let anyone bring you down. You got to keep going." - Chantal Sutherland.

"Productivity is about looking forward. Looking back too much, or trying to be too much of a perfectionist, can destroy that productivity. So keep going. Keep creating. Keep building."
