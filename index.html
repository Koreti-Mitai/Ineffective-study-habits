Studying

Ineffective study habits are the things that stop people from learning and succeeding. Ineffective study habits occur for many reasons such as distractions due to social media, lack of motivation, no routine or study plan, multitasking and procrastination etc. Another main reason ineffective study habits may occur is due to the lack of knowledge of not understanding how to study or what to study, it may also be because of the amount of work being set to students and adults which causes them to stress. It is important that these ineffective study habits are addressed and helpful so that students and adults are able to effectively learn without any barriers holding them back.
